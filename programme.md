# Programme

:::: {#schedule .section}
Talks will take place in room **TL325** in the Learning and Teaching Building on the University of Strathclyde campus, with coffee breaks and lunches in the adjacent room TL328 (TL324 on Friday).
Invited talks (with [yellow background]{.bg-warning} below) are 45 minutes long, and contributed talks are either 15 minutes ([light gray background]{style="background-color: #f2f2f2;"} below) or 20 minutes long.
The talks that are part of the [Women in EPN](https://europroofnet.github.io/women-epn-2025/) event on Tuesday afternoon have different timings.

The programme is also available in [PDF format](files/programme-TYPES2025.pdf) and as an [ICS file](files/programme-TYPES2025.ics) which you can import into your calendar (or [look at
online](https://calendar-sharing.nagyv.com/?cal=aHR0cHM6Ly9tc3AuY2lzLnN0cmF0aC5hYy51ay90eXBlczIwMjUvcHJvZ3JhbW1lLVRZUEVTMjAyNS5pY3M=)).
