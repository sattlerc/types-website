Social events
=============

### Reception

We will offer a simple reception on **Monday 4th of May** at **17:00** (immediately after the last talk) in the entrance hall of the Lindholmen Conference Center.

### Excursion

On **Tuesday 5th of May**, after a coffee break together, we will have a two-hour guided tour in the beautiful archipelago of Gothenburg on board the classic archipelago boat M/S S:t Erik.
We will leave Lindholmenspiren at **16:00** (sharp, so make sure to be there at **15:45**) and return to Lindholmenspiren at **18:20** or to [Lilla Bommen harbor](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Lilla+Bommens+Hamn/@57.7118251,11.9476005,15z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m6!3m5!1s0x464ff3610ab61401:0x8755b72283d5ac25!8m2!3d57.711815!4d11.9660329!16s%2Fg%2F12yygk_g7?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDkyMS4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D) at **18:40**.

There is a café and bar on the boat where you can buy drinks or something to eat.

### Conference dinner

The conference dinner on **Thursday 7th of May at 18:30** will take place at [Wijkanders](https://wijkanders.se/en/) located at [Vera Sandersbergs allé 5B](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Wijkanders/@57.6926316,11.9571294,15z/data=!3m2!4b1!5s0x464ff37369787c39:0xf5fc7ce3442daded!4m6!3m5!1s0x464ff3736be94497:0x57820489e5a0243a!8m2!3d57.6926215!4d11.9755618!16s%2Fg%2F11cncd4bbc?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDkyNC4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D).
To get there, take tram 10 from Lindholmen to Kapellplatsen (ca. 20 min).
From there, it is just a 3-minute walk.
