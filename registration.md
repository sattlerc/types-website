# Registration

::: {#registration .section}
Registration is now closed.
The registration fees were as follows:

- **Early regular registration (until 12 May):** £300
- **Early student registration (until 12 May):** £250
- **Late regular registration (until 30 May):** £360
- **Late student registration (until 30 May):** £310

Researchers from [COST Inclusiveness Target Countries](https://www.cost.eu/about/strategy/excellence-and-inclusiveness/) are reminded that the [European Research Network on Formal Proofs COST action](https://europroofnet.github.io/) has some [funding available for conference attendance](https://europroofnet.github.io/grants/), with a deadline of 4 May.
:::

::: {#visa .section}
Visa information

Note that participants from outside the UK (including the EU) most likely will [need a visa or an electronic travel authorisation (ETA)](https://www.gov.uk/check-uk-visa).
An ETA should be easy to apply for, but you need to do it at least a few days before travelling.

If you need to apply for a visa rather than an ETA, we are happy to supply a support letter for you after you have registered for the conference.
Please contact <CIS_types2025@groups.strath.ac.uk> with the following information:

- Your name (as it appears in your passport);
- Your date of birth;
- Your nationality;
- Your passport number;
- The dates you will be travelling to and from the UK, and from/to where.
:::
