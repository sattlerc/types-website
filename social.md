# Social Events

32nd International Conference on Types for Proofs and Programs\
4--8 May 2026, Gothenburg, Sweden, <https://types2026.cse.chalmers.se/>

::: {#excursion .section}
### Excursion

<img src="images/ms-st-erik.webp" alt="M/S S:t Erik" title="M/S S:t Erik" 
width="40%" height="40%">

On **Tuesday 5th of May** we will have a 2.5 hours guided tour in the
beautiful archipelago of Gothenburg on board of the classic
archipelago boat M/S S:t Erik. \
We will leave Lindholmenspiren at **16:00** (sharp) and return at
Lindholmenspiren at **18:30** or at [Lilla Bommen
harbor](https://www.google.com/maps/place/Lilla+Bommens+Hamn/@57.7118251,11.9476005,15z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m6!3m5!1s0x464ff3610ab61401:0x8755b72283d5ac25!8m2!3d57.711815!4d11.9660329!16s%2Fg%2F12yygk_g7?entry=ttu&g_ep=EgoyMDI1MDkyMS4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D)
at **18:45**. \
There will be no service on the boat, but we’ll enjoy a coffee break
together before we set off. 
:::
